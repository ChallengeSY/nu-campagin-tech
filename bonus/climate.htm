Climate calculator Instructions

This program will provide detailed specifications for a specific climate. Population growth is broken into steps in terms of the number of clans needed to grow a specific number (up to a certain amount as defined in configuration).

These numbers assume the following:

  • The colonists are not taxed (except Academy, which fixes colonist taxes at 5%). Taxation slows growth
  • There are no amorphous natives present. Otherwise, subtract 5 from all the growth counts. Subtract 1 more for each percentage of amorphous happiness below 90%
  • Supplies are not being factored into the maximums
