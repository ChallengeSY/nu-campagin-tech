News

Parts Builder available and other materials organized (January 18, 2017)

As requested by a fellow Planets Nu player, a Parts Builder has been released as bonus material.

Also, to make further bonus materials easier, previous bonus materials have been moved to a dedicated directory.

We hope you enjoyed the new year so far!

Ship reports overhauled! (August 22, 2017)

We have given the ship reports a major overhaul. Rather than coloring five pieces of detail based on their appearance in Planets 5, segments are instead colored based on any differences in between an upgraded design and the original design. Naturally, if an original design is selected, no text is colored (except ability links).

We also have information from PlanetsCon 2017 that Cloak and Intercept, a Bird technology, will be made Standard issue soon. Although we are early compared to official implementation, we are including it in this update anyway.

Ionic Merge Labs available (July 24, 2017)

As part of a quest to learn an intentional difference with respect to merging ion storms, we have released a new tool for everyone to play around.

Climate calculator has also received a small update: It now includes the population required to grow one less than the specified target.
